A little bit about me

My journey into tech was not the most traditional. I started with a career in education for a major museum in Los Angeles, CA. During my time there, I realized what truly motivates me is sharing unique stories that empower and engage people. I wanted to reach more, create greater impact, and connect people to the amazing world around them. How could I do this? THE INTERNET!

After learning some basic coding skills, I was hooked on being able to give a platform to anyone with a unique story to tell. When I learned how to build websites, I realized I found something that gave me purpose, as well as the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made the switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices

Ease Converting Designs into Code
